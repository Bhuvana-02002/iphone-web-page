About

The iPhone 15 Pro is a smartphone that was announced by Apple on September 12, 2023, with pre-orders starting on September 15 and availability starting on September 22. It has a titanium design that is the same as the alloy used in spacecraft. The Pro is Apple's lightest Pro lineup ever because it has one of the highest strength-to-weight ratios of any metal.

Features

The iPhone 15 Pro has many features, including:

  • Display: 6.10-inch (1179x2556)
  • Display Processor: Apple A17 Pro
  • Front camera: 12MP
  • Rear camera: 48MP + 12MP + 12MP
  • RAM: 8GB
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB
  • OS: iOS 17
  • Wireless charging: Qi2 magnetic wireless charging standard
  • Video recording: 4K video recording at 24 fps, 25 fps, 30 fps, or 60 fps(1179x2556)
  • Camera: High-quality 4K Apple ProRes video in Log color profile.
  • Sensors: Face ID, LiDAR Scanner, Barometer, High dynamic range gyro, High-g accelerometer, Proximity sensor, Dual ambient light sensors
